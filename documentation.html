"Coming up with ideas that integrate both sports and suspense can be quite challenging as it doesn't include the typical emotion: fear."

"I was eventually able to come up with a suspensful ball game that involved manipulating the readers emotions by adding twists and unexpected moments that can trigger anxiety and lots of anticipation. Unlike the scary uncertainty in horror stories, my podcast generates suspense by making readers emotionally connect with a team, making each victory feel earned and each setback deeply felt. This gets the reader invested into my writing and this way, the suspense does not come from fear but from the hope and desire to win against all odds." - Tony

Challenges Faced

"Tony and I have been working on this project for about two months, and T&J Podcasts is what we have created. Throughout this process, I encountered some challenges and roadblocks that I had to overcome to make this presentation possible. " - Josh
Firstly, at the start of the project, I had almost finished my 11 minutes of dialogue over the span of three weeks. One day, I went to complete it, and my dialogue had been deleted by another student. Though it was frustrating, I needed to finish my assignment before it was due, so Tony and I took some time to go to my house and complete it in the furthest parts of the house to avoid affecting the sound quality.
Secondly, when I was recording my story, I kept forgetting sentences or losing my place on the page, which forced me to restart the approximately one minute of dialogue that I had recorded. It was also very distracting when Tony came up to me and tried to make me laugh by tickling me and throwing pillows at me; that alone probably set me back a whole hour of recording.
Lastly, while recording my story, the words I had written in my head didn’t sound quite right when spoken aloud, so I had to improvise part of my story halfway through. This required me to rewind part of the dialogue and change it. Some of these were last-minute changes, which pressed me for time and took away about 25 minutes of work.
"Throughout the recording process, there were definitely some struggles. In the environment where I recorded the audio in, it frustrated me due to the inconsistency of the audio quality." - Tony
"Another challenge that I encountered during recording was my contrary voice. This usually is because of long hours of nonstop recording as I get tired of speaking." - Tony
"I was truly motivated to complete this podcast because I found this assignment very intriguing, and I wanted to finish it in its entirety to see the best result. Despite the month of work that went to waste, I completed it..." - Josh
