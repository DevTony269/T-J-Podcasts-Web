Our Writing Process

"When writing the story and script for the podcast, I faced many difficulties, such as problems with character development, especially because we have to engage the audience at the start of the podcast. This makes it harder to fit things in just the right spots for a smooth flow in the story." - Tony
"The motivation behind actually completing the script and the podcast itself is because of my passion for basketball, as it helped me a lot with rendering realistic situations that would be set on a real court." - Tony "The game itself is based on a true experience, as I was inspired by Marshall Spring's game against Midsun in the 2025 Junior Boy Basketball Division Finals."

Our Recording Process

"Throughout the recording process, there were definitely some struggles. In the environment where I recorded the audio in, it frustrated me due to the inconsistency of the audio quality." - Tony
"Another challenge that I encountered during recording was my contrary voice. This usually is because of long hours of nonstop recording as I get tired of speaking." - Tony
"I was truly motivated to complete this podcast because I found this assignment very intriguing, and I wanted to finish it in its entirety to see the best result. Despite the month of work that went to waste, I completed it..." - Josh
